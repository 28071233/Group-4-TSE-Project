Step 1

Our first step is installing the CPU. To do this, the CPU retention bracket is lifted so we can gain access to the CPU socket Next, we carefully hold the CPU by it's sides and allign the triangle in the corner with the one on the socket. Afterwards, we place the cpu into the socket and give it a little wiggle to ensure it's securely in place. Finally, we close the retention bracket, ensuring that the cpu is secured to the motherboard.
